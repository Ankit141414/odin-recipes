Pizza Recipes!

The most famous and favourite snack of people all around the world enjoyed in various ways!

Don't let it cool down because everywhere in world pizza galore , when it's served hot, it's what i adore.

Created from scratch through tomato sauce and baked in oven, pizza is heaven to our taste buds. While the consistency of things within pizza is much, it's not so hard to create it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onions
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 100 gm processed cheese
  • 4 mushroom
  • 1/2 capsicum
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water

Steps to follow

  1. Prepare the pizza dough and add salt and baking powder in it then make a well in the center and add 1 teaspoon of oil then add water and yeast and mix it
  2. Prepare the pizza base
  3. Chop the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Bake the pizza at 250 degree celcius for 10 minures

Now the pizza is ready to serve! YUMMMMMM!
