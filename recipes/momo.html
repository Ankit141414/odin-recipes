Momo Recipes!

A delicious lunch within the Nepalese, Tibetian and Indian land best served with chutney and ketchup.

One of the favourite meal of not only me but millions of Nepalese !

Momo can be made any way you like but the main theme is chicken, buff and veg. We can either steam it or fry it. A fantastic snack which can be made under 30 minutes for your whole family.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 pounds ground chicken thighs(or cabbage and soybean if you like veg)
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 4 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 4 table spoons minced peeled ginger
  • 2 table spoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinammon
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Steps to follow

  1. Mix the flour and 1 1/2 cups pf water in a bowl Knead the dough and rest it for 1 hour.
  2. Mix together the chicken,onions,garlic, ginger cumin,2 tablespoons salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper in a bowl.
  3. Make wrappers by breaking off 1/2 ounce of dough and forming it into a 4-inch round with a rolling pin. Repeat.
  4. Spray a steamer pan with cooking spray.
  5. place a tablespoon of the chicken filling in the middle od the wrapper and wrap it. Place the momo in the prepared steamer pan. Repeat.
  6. Fill the stramer pot halfway with water and bring it to boil. Set the steamer pan with the momos on top of the pot and cover with tight lid. Steam the momos until cooked, 8 to 9 minutes.
